  - stack points to `buildPublicFileUrl`

- `MINIO_PUBLIC_URL` comes from `process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_MINIO_ENDPOINT` in [minio.ts](/home/dmytro/Github/phd-work/nextcrm-app-update/lib/minio.ts:19).
- Both local env files currently have `NEXT_PUBLIC_MINIO_ENDPOINT=` with no value.

Why this is the root cause:
- The route validates required request fields and content type before reaching the failing block.
- The server-side MinIO variables are checked at module load, but `NEXT_PUBLIC_MINIO_ENDPOINT` is not checked.
